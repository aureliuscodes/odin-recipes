Bolognese recipe

Traditional Italian bolognese with heritage from Tuscany topped with fresh basil

Ingredients

  • 2 shallots
  • Carrots
  • Cherry/cocktail tomatoes
  • Olive oil
  • 350 grams of minced meat
  • Tomato paste
  • Beef stocking
  • Cloves
  • Red Wine
  • Linguine 250g

Steps

  1. Chop shallots, carrots and tomatoes into fine pieces
  2. Season the minced meat with salt, pepper, olive oil
  3. Fry shallots and carrots at medium heat until hints of browning and add the tomato paste and tomatoes
  4. Add minced meat and fry until golden brown-ish
  5. Season the meat with cloves and beef stocking
  6. Add a modest amount of red wine
  7. Put the the pasta water to boil and salt well
  8. Add the linguine pasta and cook until al dente (approx. 8 min)
