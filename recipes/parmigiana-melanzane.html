Parmigiana Melanzane recipe

Delicious Parmigiana Melanzane with a fresh and unique taste

Ingredients

  • Eggplant (2 medium-sized)
  • Tomato sauce (2 cups)
  • Mozzarella cheese (1 cup, shredded)
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (1/2 cup)
  • Flour (1/2 cup)
  • Eggs (2 large)
  • Breadcrumbs (1 cup)
  • Vegetable oil (for frying)
  • Fresh basil leaves (for garnish)
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Wash and slice the eggplants into 1/4-inch thick rounds.
  3. In separate bowls, place the flour, beaten eggs, and breadcrumbs.
  4. Season the eggplant slices with salt and pepper.
  5. Dip each eggplant slice into the flour, then the beaten eggs, and finally the breadcrumbs, ensuring they are evenly coated.
  6. In a large skillet, heat vegetable oil over medium-high heat.
  7. Fry the breaded eggplant slices in batches until they are golden brown and crispy on both sides. Place them on a paper towel-lined plate to remove excess oil.
  8. In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of tomato sauce on the bottom.
  9. Add a layer of fried eggplant slices on top of the sauce.
  10. Sprinkle a portion of shredded mozzarella and grated Parmesan cheese over the eggplant layer.
  11. Repeat the layers of sauce, eggplant, and cheese until all the ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of cheese on top.
  12. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  13. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10 minutes or until the top is golden brown.
  14. Let it cool for a few minutes before serving.
  15. Garnish with fresh basil leaves.
  16. Enjoy your delicious Parmigiana Melanzane!
