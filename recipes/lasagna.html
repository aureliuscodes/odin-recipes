Homemade Italian Lasagna

Unbeatable lasagna with a original taste of Piedmonte

  • Lasagna noodles (12 pieces)
  • Ground beef (1 pound)
  • Italian sausage (1/2 pound)
  • Onion (1 medium, finely chopped)
  • Garlic cloves (3, minced)
  • Crushed tomatoes (28 oz can)
  • Tomato sauce (15 oz can)
  • Tomato paste (6 oz can)
  • Water (1/2 cup)
  • Fresh basil leaves (a handful, chopped)
  • Fresh parsley (a handful, chopped)
  • Ricotta cheese (15 oz container)
  • Egg (1 large)
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese (3 cups)
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (1 cup)
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)
  • Olive oil (2 tablespoons)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large pot, boil water and cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a separate large skillet, heat olive oil over medium-high heat.
  4. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, sauté until fragrant and translucent.
  5. Add the ground beef and Italian sausage to the skillet, breaking them into small pieces with a spoon. Cook until browned and cooked through.
  6. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, and tomato paste. Add water to the sauce to reach the desired consistency.
  7. Season the sauce with chopped basil, parsley, salt, and pepper. Let it simmer for 15-20 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.
  8. In a medium bowl, mix the ricotta cheese with the egg and a pinch of salt and pepper.
  9. Spread a thin layer of the meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  10. Arrange a layer of cooked lasagna noodles over the sauce.
  11. Spoon half of the ricotta cheese mixture over the noodles and spread it evenly.
  12. Sprinkle a portion of shredded mozzarella and grated Parmesan cheese over the ricotta layer.
  13. Repeat the layers with meat sauce, noodles, ricotta cheese, and cheese until all the ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of cheese on top.
  14. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  15. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10 minutes or until the top is golden brown and bubbling.
  16. Let the lasagna cool for a few minutes before serving.
  17. Enjoy your delicious Homemade Lasagna!
